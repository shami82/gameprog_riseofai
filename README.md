# Meet Me There (Platformer)

## Controls
- Zorp (Player)
  - [W] to jump up
  - [A] to move left
  - [D] to move right
- Other
  - [Enter] to Start/Restart the game (only at Start/Lose/End screens)

## Rules
- Make it to your rocket ship to meet your friend
- Avoid enemies and obstacles
- You have to make it to the end with 3 lives
- Health is shown at the top left of the screen

## Misc
- 3 Enemies/Obstacles
  - UFO Flyers
  - Alien Wanderers
  - Falling Planet Platforms
